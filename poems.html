Sensitive

She’s a sensitive girl, quiet and kind,

Her heart’s an ocean, with waves hard to find.

People say she’s too much, she feels too deep,

But they don’t know the secrets she keeps.

She just needs to be seen, to be held with care,

Not judged for the weight of the feelings she bears.

She’s waiting for love, not harsh words or regret.

She hasn’t been understood or loved right yet.

- Fit

A Love That Speaks

He holds her hand,

so soft, so near,

A silent promise,

crystal clear.

In every touch,

a world is found,

A love that speaks

without a sound.

- Fit
